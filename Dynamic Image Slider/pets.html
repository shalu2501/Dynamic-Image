DOG
Dogs (Canis lupus familiaris) are domesticated mammals known for their loyalty, intelligence, and companionship.
CAT
Cats are small, carnivorous mammals known for their agility, sharp senses, and independent nature.
FISH
Fish are cold-blooded aquatic animals that live in water and breathe through gills.
RABBIT
Rabbits are small mammals known for their long ears, fluffy tails, and powerful hind legs.
HAMSTER
A hamster is a small, furry rodent often kept as a pet. They are known for their chubby cheeks, short tails, and playful behavior. Hamsters are clean, quiet, and easy to care for.
PARROT
A parrot is a colorful, intelligent bird known for its ability to mimic sounds and human speech.
