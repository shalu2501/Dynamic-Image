ROSE
A rose is a type of flowering plant known for its beauty, fragrance, and symbolic meaning. It belongs to the plant family Rosaceae
SUNFLOWER
A sunflower is a tall, bright yellow flowering plant known for turning its face toward the sun. It belongs to the plant family Asteraceae.
LILY
A lily is a beautiful, often fragrant flowering plant. It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is known for its large, colorful, trumpet-shaped flowers.
TULIP
A tulip is a brightly colored, cup-shaped flower that blooms in spring. It belongs to the Liliaceae family, the same family as lilies.
DAISY
A daisy is a simple, cheerful flower with white petals and a yellow center. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, one of the largest plant families in the world.
LOTUS
The lotus is a beautiful, water-loving flower that grows in ponds and lakes. It is a symbol of purity and peace in many cultures and religions. The lotus belongs to the plant family Nelumbonaceae.
