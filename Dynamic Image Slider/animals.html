TIGER
A tiger is a large, powerful wild cat known for its orange fur with black stripes. It is the largest member of the cat family (Felidae).
BEAR
A bear is a large, strong mammal known for its thick fur, sharp claws, and strong limbs. Bears are omnivores, meaning they eat both plants and animals.
DEER
A deer is a herbivorous mammal known for its slender legs, gentle nature, and (in most species) antlers found on males.
LION
A lion is a large, powerful wild cat known as the "King of the Jungle" (though it mostly lives in grasslands). Lions are carnivorous mammals that live in social groups called prides.
CHEETAH
The cheetah is a large, spotted wild cat known as the fastest land animal on Earth.
HORES
A horse is a large, strong herbivorous mammal known for its speed, endurance, and close relationship with humans
