FLOWERS
A flower is the reproductive part of a flowering plant (angiosperm).It is often brightly colored and fragrant to attract pollinators like bees, birds, and butterflies.

NATURE
Nature includes all the living and non-living things found in the environment that are not made by humans.

PETS
Pets are animals that people keep for companionship, fun, and love. They are usually friendly, tame, and live with humans at home.

ANIMALS
Animals are living organisms that belong to the kingdom Animalia. They are multicellular, eukaryotic (have a nucleus in their cells), and they typically move, eat, and respond to their environment.
SLIDER
BIRDS
Birds are warm-blooded, egg-laying animals with feathers, wings, and beaks. They are a group of vertebrates (animals with backbones) classified under the class Aves.
SLIDER
BUTTERFLY
Butterflies are colorful insects known for their beautiful wings and graceful flight. They belong to the order Lepidoptera, which also includes moths.
